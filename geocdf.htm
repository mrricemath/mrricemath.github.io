This applet computes probabilities for the geometric distribution

X∼Geo(p)X∼Geo(p)
where
X=the number of failures before the 1st successX=the number of failures before the 1st success

Directions

  • Enter the probability of success in the pp box.
  • Hitting "Tab" or "Enter" on your keyboard will plot the probability mass function (pmf).
